History of AI

  • Back in the 1800’s, AI was limited in myths and fiction​
  • A workshop in Dartmoth College was the seed that led towards an AI future​
  • The surge of AI began with six major design goals​
  • The funding for this workshop was soon closed​
  • The 21st century brought the concept back quickly

Pros of AI

  • New innovative technology that benefits and improves the world today
  • Improves efficiency in certain fields ex. Environmental problems and medical problems
  • Makes things more accessible and easier such as websites and software (tutorials and step by step introduction​
  • Faster actions and decisions

Cons of AI

  • The prices to build up an AI is a lot, even though it has been decreasing throughout the years.​
  • A lot of people would be losing their jobs because of AI​
  • Can lead to bad calls, AI does not have the ability to make judgement calls 
  • The translations between these machines may be inaccurate if not reviewed by humans​
  • There could be unstableness or malfunctioning depending on how the machine was made and manufactured.​
